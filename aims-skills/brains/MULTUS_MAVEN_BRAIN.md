## AI Deployment Readiness Assessment (Lead Magnet)

10-question diagnostic that evaluates whether a company is ready to move AI from pilot to production:

1. What stage is your AI initiative? (Exploration / Pilot / Scaling / Production)
2. Do you have a dedicated AI/ML engineering team? (Yes, >5 / Yes, <5 / No, using generalists / No team)
3. Is your training data documented, versioned, and accessible? (Fully / Partially / No)
4. Do you have CI/CD pipelines for model deployment? (Yes, automated / Manual process / No)
5. What's your monitoring strategy for deployed models? (Full observability / Basic logging / None)
6. Are you subject to compliance requirements? (SOC2/HIPAA/FedRAMP / Industry-specific / None)
7. What's your model serving infrastructure? (Dedicated ML platform / Custom / None)
8. Do you have a rollback strategy for model failures? (Automated / Manual / No)
9. What's your AI budget allocation? (>$500K / $100K-$500K / <$100K / Unknown)
10. How many AI use cases are you targeting? (10+ / 5-10 / 1-5 / Exploring)

**Scoring:** Each answer maps to a readiness tier (Production-Ready / Scaling / Pilot / Exploration).
Personalized report with specific recommendations for each gap identified.
